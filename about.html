About Us

Learn more about EcoXplore Adventures and our mission

Our Story

EcoXplore Adventures was founded in 2018 by a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts who wanted to share their love for nature with the world — responsibly.

We started with a simple idea: adventure tourism doesn't have to harm the environment. With that vision, we built a company that offers exciting outdoor experiences while protecting the natural spaces we all love.

Today, we operate hiking, camping, cycling, and river rafting tours across some of the most stunning landscapes, always keeping sustainability at the core of everything we do.

Our Values

Sustainability

We plan every tour with minimal environmental impact, using eco-friendly equipment and green practices.

Community

We support local businesses and hire local guides to benefit the communities we visit.

Safety

Your safety is our top priority. All guides are certified and trained in first aid and wilderness rescue.

Experience

We craft unique, memorable experiences tailored to all adventure levels — beginner to expert.

Our Environmental Commitment

We take our responsibility to the environment seriously. Here is how we give back:

  • We plant one tree for every booking made
  • All waste from tours is collected and properly disposed of
  • We only use biodegradable camping and cleaning products
  • 10% of all profits go to local conservation projects
  • We operate in small groups to reduce our environmental footprint
